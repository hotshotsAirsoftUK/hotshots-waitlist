The Experience

Hotshots is a fully mobile airsoft experience that comes to you. We bring professional-grade equipment, digital scoring systems, and a structured competitive format directly to your venue, event, or location. No range membership needed — we set everything up on your turf.

Two formats. Static Shooting Range — precision-focused, built on the AttackSense digital system, real-time leaderboard scoring, ideal for competitive accuracy challenges. Tactical CQB — dynamic and high-intensity, mixing steel poppers with AttackSense digital targets, designed around IPSC-style competitive shooting scenarios. Both run with live scoring and a professional debrief.

Sessions are available in 2, 4, or 8-hour blocks. The right length depends on your group size and event type — we'll advise you during booking.

Sessions are led by Nana, a serving British Army Reserve Royal Engineer and qualified Skill at Arms Instructor. He's trained military units — your session benefits from that same level of structure, safety awareness, and attention to detail.
